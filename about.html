What
is it?

This website allows you to play Tic Tac Toe with your mobile phone, remotely.
With the help of your phone's accelerometer, we can capture your movements and let you freely play the game.
In the "Spectate" menu, you may also watch another player's game, that is currently being played.

How
to play?

Just connect with your phone, and a new player will appear in the "Connected players" list. Select two players from this list, and click the button "Start game". A game will start.
The turn is indicated at the top of the board (the first player selected is X, and the second one is O), and after a player wins, a window will popup with the winning player. To start a new game, just click the "Start game" button again.
To spectate a game, choose a game from the "Current games" list. You will be able to start watching the game right away.
